    About "CareerCraft"

    A large segment of India’s potential workforce is unemployable. With 76.04% literacy rate and an increasing number of dropouts failing to enrol or complete any form of higher education, the situation is worrying. Financial constraints, lack of know-how about education funding schemes are some of the key contributors to this effect. Buddy4Study, since 2011, is endeavoring to bridge the gap between scholarship providers and scholarship seekers. As India’s largest scholarship listing portal, we help more than 1 million students by connecting the right scholarships with the right students. Backed by its robust scholarship search engine, it is the only platform in the country that allows both seekers and scholarship providers to access curated scholarship information across the globe.
